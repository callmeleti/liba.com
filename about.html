Welcome to LIBA

LIBA is a premier educational institution dedicated to nurturing young minds and preparing them for the challenges of tomorrow. Founded in 2011, we have been at the forefront of academic excellence and holistic development.

We believe in fostering creativity, critical thinking, and character development alongside academic achievement.

Academic Excellence

Consistently ranked among the top schools in the region for academic performance.

Inclusive Community

We celebrate diversity and foster an environment of mutual respect and understanding.

Innovative Learning

Cutting-edge teaching methodologies that prepare students for the future.

Character Building

Developing ethical leaders with strong moral values and social responsibility.

Our Mission & Vision

At LIBA, we are guided by a clear mission and vision that shapes everything we do:

Our Mission

To provide a transformative educational experience that empowers students to achieve their full potential, fosters a love for lifelong learning, and prepares them to make meaningful contributions to society.

Our Vision

To be recognized as a global leader in education, known for developing innovative, compassionate, and intellectually curious individuals who will shape a better future for all.
